INTERNET INFRASTRUCTURE IN WELLINGTON

A look into some of the many hints to internet infrastructure scattered around the city of Wellington.

The physical infrasturcture to internet access and the people who provide it to us.

TYPES OF INTERNET INFRASTRUCTURE:

TRANSPORT NETWORKING AROUND THE HOUSE INTERNET PROVIDERS

Transport Networking

Infrastructure related to better assisting the use of transport throughout the Wellington City

ANTENNAS

Companies such as metlink, use an antena to connect the expected arrival of a bus, to the digital screen for the convience of their customers.

TRAFFIC LIGHTS

Traffic lights that are connectted to an online system that corrects timers and makes sure commuters are not stuck in traffic.

INTERACTIVE BUS STOP

A QR code at a bus stop can provide people with more information regarding a bus company or timetable simply by scanning the QR Code with their phone.

Around The House

Examples of objects which use the internet or connect us to it to benefit our everyday lives.

FIBRE BOX

This fibre box connects to our router providing our flat with ultra fast fibre optic broadband.

ROUTER

The Router connects us all to the internet by providing us with WiFi around our flat.

LAPTOP

My Laptop can connect to the WiFi meaning that I am able to connect to the internet.

PHONE

Using my phone I am also able to connect to the internet using WiFi and also cellular data provided by my phone company.

Internet Providers

People who provide internet access to the people of Wellington

CHORUS VAN

A Chorus workers van that transports them to help repair and connect our city to the internet.

CHORUS BOX

This Chorus box provides a building in the CBD with fibre optic internet and helps buisnesses stay connected.

SPARK

Spark provides the option for their customers to access free WiFi around the city of Wellington by placing routers atop of these telephone boxes.

2DEGREES

2Degrees is a telephone provider that allows their customers to purchase mobile Data to use when they are on the go and are not in a accessible WiFi zone.
